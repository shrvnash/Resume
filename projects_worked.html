  3. Project #3

    Title: Mitogen Genetics 6.2 and 6.3(FAST and Lab)

    Technologies: MTM, TFS.

    Role: Quality Engineer

    Description:

    Filtration, Annotation, and Storage Tool (FAST) is a tool for managing, annotating and filtering large data sets from tests such as large panels,
    exomes, and genomes. The system facilitates the determination of which variants from a case are the most clinically relevant and will ultimately be
    reported. A laboratory can load both sample (patient) data and annotation (external information from publicly and privately available databases) data
    into FAST. The sample and annotation data are then combined to allow systematic curation and filtering of the identified variants. The laboratory can
    filter this data using saved filters and/or ad hoc filters for searching. Once the laboratory has identified the final set of variants for reporting,
    they are either downloaded or sent to Sunquest Mitogenâ„¢ Genetics Lab for reporting. Lab 6.2 is designed to assist you in drafting molecular diagnostic
    test reports. It also assists in tracking data used as input in the report generation process. Reports generated by Mitogen genetics Lab must be reviewed
    and approved for accuracy by experienced, trained and properly credentialed medical personnel prior to release for clinical use.
